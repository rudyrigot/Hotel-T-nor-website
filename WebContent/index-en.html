A hotel in the heart of the city

Breakfast in the summertime

Sa situation exceptionnelle offre la proximité immédiate des musées, galeries et autres manifestations culturelles, permet de visiter à son rythme les différentes curiosités de la ville. From 7am, you may choose to enjoy your breakfast in the quietness of your room, in the comfort of our breakfast room, or relaxing in our suspended garden. Quality products are available.

Nights out nearby

Whether you're after a movie night at the Méliès or Gaumont theaters (3 minutes away), or eager to discover the bars and restaurants in the old town (5 minutes away), you'll be right in the heart of the "stéphanoise" life !
